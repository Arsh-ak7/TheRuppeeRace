Welcome to the Official Website of The Rupee Race Podcast. Growing up, we were huge fans of Joe Rogan. If you don't know who he is, he is a comedian, podcaster, and UFC anchor. He has a podcast called "The Joe Rogan Experience", where he'd invite people from tech, health, and other niches to talk about their experiences and their opinions. we wanted to do the same but in a different niche.

Team: Javeed has co-founded the venture with 2 other members, Arnav Shindolkar and Ayush Pawar who are as passionate as he is and have a much bigger perspective of life than he does. Having their perspective adds a lot to the podcast. We also have someone who is very ardent about finding answers, Shishir Rao is a 3rd Year Student at the same college we study. He is the Head of content writing. Also! We have two New Additions to the Team. Arsh Kumar is Our Technical Head and Samarth as Our Creative Head.
MIssion: We have always wanted to introduce students to this form of broadcasting media. There are a lot of students in India who are confused about what to do in their life and which path to choose. This podcast is exactly for that purpose. We would invite and talk with students whose life journeys are worth telling. We plan on not restricting ourselves to just success stories but stories of failures as well because that is where we learn from. We have realized the importance of finance and self-help. Every individual should know how to manage their finances. We are also venturing out to invite entrepreneurs and people with good management of finances so that they could teach us a thing or two about how to Invest, Save and Multiply the Money.
